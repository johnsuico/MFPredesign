How To Help

Volunteer or Donate

Volunteer

The best way to schedule volunteer time is to email us with the desired date you'd like to come in.


Our hours are Monday through Thursday from 9 Am to 1 PM, Thursday evening from 6 PM to 8PM, and saturday from 9 AM to 12 noon.


At this time we are not accepting court-ordered community service. Thank you for understanding.


Email: volunteer@milpitasfoodpantry.org

Donate Food or Clothing

We appreciate the support of the community!


You may drop off donations of food or clothing during our regular hours of operation.


Monday - Thursday 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM

Thursday Evening 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM

Saturday 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM

Closed Friday & Sunday
